Vijay

Joseph Vijay Chandrasekhar (born 22 June 1974), known as Vijay, is a top Indian actor, playback singer, and politician in Tamil cinema. Known as "Thalapathy" (Commander), he has starred in 69 films over three decades. He made his debut at age 18 in Naalaiya Theerpu (1992) and launched his political party, Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam, in 2024.
